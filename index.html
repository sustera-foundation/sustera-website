About Sustera Foundation

Sustera Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit organization dedicated to empowering communities by identifying and implementing local actions that build resilience. Our work focuses on capacity building, outreach, and advocacy, contributing to the achievement of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN-SDGs) for 2030.

Our Story

Our journey started with a mission to make communities resilient to climate challenges. Over the years, we have evolved, focusing on education, empowerment, and sustainable development.

Team, Vision & Mission

Our vision is a resilient and sustainable future for all. Our team is committed to driving this vision forward through collaborative efforts and strategic initiatives.
